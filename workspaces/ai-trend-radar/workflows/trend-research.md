# Trend Research Workflow

## 1. Purpose

Research a specific AI trend and convert evidence into product, technical, design, and business implications.

## 2. When to Use

Use when a topic needs deeper investigation than daily or weekly radar.

## 3. Inputs

- Research topic
- Research question
- Timeframe
- Source constraints
- Product domain
- Desired output depth

## 4. Steps

1. Define the research question.
2. Generate search keywords from technology, product category, user problem, companies, and use cases.
3. Select sources by credibility and relevance.
4. Classify claims as fact, opinion, or speculation.
5. Cross-validate important claims with multiple sources.
6. Analyze product, technical, design, and business impact.
7. Extract opportunities, risks, open questions, and actions.

## 5. Outputs

- Research question
- Source list
- Evidence table
- Fact / opinion / speculation classification
- Product implications
- Technical implications
- Design implications
- Business implications
- Opportunities
- Risks
- Recommended actions

## 6. Quality Checklist

- Research question is specific.
- Source credibility is visible.
- Claims are classified.
- Important claims are cross-validated.
- Actions are concrete and workspace-aware.

## 7. Common Mistakes

- Starting with a conclusion.
- Using only community sources.
- Not separating facts from opinions.
- Missing design or business implications.
- Producing insight without action.
