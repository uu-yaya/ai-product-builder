# AI Weekly Radar Workflow

## 1. Purpose

Summarize one week of AI signals into trends, opportunities, demo ideas, learning plan, and hype check.

## 2. When to Use

Use for weekly AI review, product planning, learning planning, or roadmap inspiration.

## 3. Inputs

- Week range
- Topics
- Companies or product categories
- Source preferences
- Product domains of interest

## 4. Steps

1. Gather product releases, technical updates, open-source projects, papers, technical blogs, YouTube / community signals, and competitor signals.
2. Cluster signals into top trends.
3. Identify evidence and counter-signals for each trend.
4. Analyze product, technical, design, and business implications.
5. Extract product opportunities and demo ideas.
6. Generate a learning plan.
7. Add risks and hype check.
8. Recommend actions and target workspaces.

## 5. Outputs

- Executive summary
- Top trends
- Product releases
- Technical updates
- Open-source projects
- Papers / research
- YouTube / community signals
- Competitor signals
- Product opportunities
- Demo ideas
- Learning plan
- Risks and hype check

## 6. Quality Checklist

- Trends are backed by multiple signals or strong primary sources.
- Facts, opinions, and speculation are separated.
- Product opportunities are specific.
- Demo ideas are buildable.
- Hype and uncertainty are visible.

## 7. Common Mistakes

- Listing every update without synthesis.
- Ignoring weak evidence.
- Missing competitor signals.
- Creating demo ideas not tied to user problems.
- Treating weekly excitement as durable trend.

## 8. Example Command

请根据 `templates/AI_WEEKLY_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md` 生成本周 AI Agent 趋势周报，包含 Top Trends、产品发布、技术更新、开源项目、论文、社区信号、产品机会、Demo ideas、学习计划和风险判断。
