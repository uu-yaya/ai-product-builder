# AI Daily Radar Workflow

## 1. Purpose

Generate a concise AI daily radar that turns important updates into PM insights and actions.

## 2. When to Use

Use when checking the last 24 hours of important AI news, product releases, research, open-source projects, and community signals.

## 3. Inputs

- Time window
- Topics or companies
- Source preferences
- Output depth
- Product domains of interest

## 4. Steps

1. Define search scope and source priority.
2. Prioritize official blogs, product release pages, GitHub, papers, trusted media, and first-hand sources.
3. Classify signals as news, product release, paper, open-source project, or community signal.
4. Deduplicate repeated coverage of the same event.
5. Judge importance by product impact, technical novelty, adoption signal, credibility, and actionability.
6. Separate facts, opinions, and speculation.
7. Convert important updates into PM insight, product opportunity, demo idea, learning notes, and actions.

## 5. Outputs

- Daily update table
- Source links and credibility
- PM insight
- Product opportunity
- Demo idea
- Learning notes
- Recommended actions

## 6. Quality Checklist

- Latest claims are verified and sourced.
- Duplicate stories are merged.
- Community claims are not treated as facts.
- Each important update has product meaning.
- Actions point to the right workspace.

## 7. Common Mistakes

- Summarizing headlines only.
- Treating one viral post as a trend.
- Missing source credibility.
- Ignoring product implications.
- Overloading the daily report with low-signal items.

## 8. Example Command

请根据 `templates/AI_DAILY_REPORT_TEMPLATE.md` 生成今天的 AI 日报，覆盖最近 24 小时重要更新，标注来源、事实、为什么重要、PM insight 和行动建议。
