## 5. Source List

| Source | Best For | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Figma Community | Design systems, templates, component organization | Learn structure, not direct visuals. |
| Dribbble | Visual mood and craft | Validate usability before adapting. |
| Mobbin | Mobile flows and product patterns | Learn flow patterns, not brand expression. |
| Awwwards | Visual storytelling and motion | Avoid sacrificing usability. |
| Behance | Case studies and visual systems | Extract principles only. |
| Pinterest | Moodboards and visual references | Use for direction, not product decisions. |
| Apple Human Interface Guidelines | Platform behavior and accessibility | Adapt to platform context. |
| Material Design | Components and interaction standards | Avoid generic look if brand needs distinction. |
