# Competitor Analysis Workflow

## 1. Purpose

Analyze competitors to identify user expectations, product gaps, differentiation opportunities, and recommended actions.

## 2. When to Use

Use when evaluating a market, product category, competitor feature, pricing model, UX pattern, or AI capability.

## 3. Inputs

- Analysis goal
- Product category
- Target users
- Competitor list or selection criteria
- Dimensions to compare
- Timeframe and source constraints

## 4. Steps

1. Define the analysis goal.
2. Select competitors using clear criteria.
3. Gather information from official sites, docs, product pages, pricing pages, reviews, demos, or trusted public sources.
4. Decompose features, user journeys, AI capabilities, UX highlights, pricing, and business model.
5. Identify learnings and what not to copy.
6. Extract differentiation opportunities.
7. Recommend product actions.

## 5. Outputs

- 分析目标
- 竞品选择标准
- 信息来源
- 功能拆解
- 用户路径拆解
- AI 能力拆解
- 商业模式
- 可借鉴点
- 不应照抄的点
- 差异化机会
- 推荐行动

## 6. Quality Checklist

- Latest external facts are verified and sourced.
- Competitor selection is justified.
- Analysis covers user journey and business model, not only features.
- Differentiation opportunities are specific.
- Recommended actions are practical.

## 7. Common Mistakes

- Listing features without insight.
- Copying competitor UX or copy.
- Ignoring pricing and distribution.
- Treating unverified claims as facts.
- Missing what not to copy.
