# prd-writer Skill Spec

## Skill Name

prd-writer

## Purpose

Turn ambiguous product ideas into clear, testable product requirements.

## Trigger Scenarios

Use when the user asks for a PRD, feature requirement, product spec, or requirement clarification.

## Inputs

- Product idea\n- Target user\n- Scenario and pain point\n- Business goal\n- Constraints\n- Existing context or references

## Workflow

1. Clarify user, scenario, pain point, goal, value, MVP, and risk.\n2. Separate assumptions from confirmed facts.\n3. Define functional and non-functional requirements.\n4. Add acceptance criteria and open questions.\n5. Keep scope focused on the next buildable version.

## Outputs

- PRD structure\n- User stories\n- Requirement list\n- Acceptance criteria\n- Risks and open questions

## Quality Checklist

- User and scenario are explicit.\n- MVP scope is clear.\n- Requirements are testable.\n- Risks and assumptions are visible.\n- Engineering handoff is actionable.

## Guardrails

- Do not over-design.\n- Do not invent business facts.\n- Do not skip AI necessity evaluation when AI features are involved.
