# ai-trend-radar Skill Spec

## Skill Name

ai-trend-radar

## Purpose

Track AI trends and turn verified external signals into product opportunities and demo ideas.

## Trigger Scenarios

Use when the user asks for AI news, GitHub Trending, YouTube insights, papers, product launches, or opportunity discovery.

## Inputs

- Research topic\n- Timeframe\n- Preferred sources\n- Product domain\n- Output format\n- Depth level

## Workflow

1. Define the research question and timeframe.\n2. Verify current information from primary or reliable sources.\n3. Cluster signals into themes.\n4. Assess product impact and timing.\n5. Generate opportunity hypotheses and demo ideas.

## Outputs

- Trend brief\n- Source list\n- Signal clusters\n- Product implications\n- Demo ideas

## Quality Checklist

- Current claims are verified.\n- Sources are linked or named.\n- Trends are separated from hype.\n- Opportunities connect to user value.\n- Demo ideas are concrete.

## Guardrails

- Do not present rumors as facts.\n- Do not rely on stale memory for current trends.\n- Do not chase novelty without product relevance.
