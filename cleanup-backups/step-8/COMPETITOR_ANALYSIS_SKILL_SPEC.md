# competitor-analysis Skill Spec

## Skill Name

competitor-analysis

## Purpose

Analyze competitors and alternatives to identify positioning, gaps, risks, and product opportunities.

## Trigger Scenarios

Use when the user asks for competitor research, market comparison, feature gap analysis, or positioning support.

## Inputs

- Product category\n- Target users\n- Competitor names or discovery criteria\n- Evaluation dimensions\n- Timeframe\n- Source constraints

## Workflow

1. Confirm the comparison goal.\n2. Gather verified source material when latest information matters.\n3. Compare positioning, features, pricing, UX, distribution, and trust signals.\n4. Extract gaps and defensible opportunities.\n5. Summarize implications for product strategy.

## Outputs

- Competitor matrix\n- Key insights\n- Opportunity areas\n- Risks\n- Recommended next moves

## Quality Checklist

- Sources are clear when current facts are used.\n- Comparison dimensions match the goal.\n- Insights go beyond feature listing.\n- Opportunities are specific and actionable.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy competitor designs or copy.\n- Do not claim latest facts without verification.\n- Do not confuse user perception with technical superiority.
