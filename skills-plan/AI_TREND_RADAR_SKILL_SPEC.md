# ai-trend-radar Skill Spec

## Skill Name

ai-trend-radar

## Purpose

Track AI trend signals and convert them into product insights, opportunities, and demo ideas.

## Trigger Scenarios

- The user asks for AI trend research, weekly radar, product release tracking, or demo ideas.
- A new AI technology, paper, tool, or product launch needs product interpretation.
- Current information may change the direction of PM, design, or engineering work.

## Inputs

- Research topic
- Timeframe
- Preferred sources
- Product domain
- Output format
- Depth level

## Workflow

1. Define the research question and timeframe.
2. Verify current information from primary or reliable sources.
3. Cluster signals into themes.
4. Assess product impact and timing.
5. Generate opportunity hypotheses and demo ideas.

## Outputs

- Trend brief
- Source list
- Signal clusters
- Product implications
- Demo ideas

## Quality Checklist

- Current claims are verified.
- Sources are linked or named.
- Trends are separated from hype.
- Opportunities connect to user value.
- Demo ideas are concrete.

## Guardrails

- Do not present rumors as facts.
- Do not rely on stale memory for current trends.
- Do not chase novelty without product relevance.
