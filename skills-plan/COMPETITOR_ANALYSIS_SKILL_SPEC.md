# competitor-analysis Skill Spec

## Skill Name

competitor-analysis

## Purpose

Compare competitors in a way that reveals strategic opportunities, not just feature lists.

## Trigger Scenarios

- The user asks to analyze competitors, alternatives, or market positioning.
- A product idea needs differentiation before PRD or MVP planning.
- Current competitor information may affect product strategy.

## Inputs

- Product category
- Target users
- Competitor names or discovery criteria
- Evaluation dimensions
- Timeframe
- Source constraints

## Workflow

1. Confirm the comparison goal.
2. Gather verified source material when latest information matters.
3. Compare positioning, features, pricing, UX, distribution, and trust signals.
4. Extract gaps and defensible opportunities.
5. Summarize implications for product strategy.

## Outputs

- Competitor matrix
- Key insights
- Opportunity areas
- Risks
- Recommended next moves

## Quality Checklist

- Sources are clear when current facts are used.
- Comparison dimensions match the goal.
- Insights go beyond feature listing.
- Opportunities are specific and actionable.

## Guardrails

- Do not copy competitor designs or copy.
- Do not claim latest facts without verification.
- Do not confuse user perception with technical superiority.
